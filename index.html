About Me

Full-Stack Developer | Angular & Laravel Enthusiast

I’m a full-stack developer with a background in Computer Engineering (IT) from Yazd University. I started by designing WordPress themes and grew into full-stack development through hands-on experience.

At WillaArts, I worked with Vue.js to build reusable components and led the front-end of lilianamodels.com. At HyvaTech, I focused on Angular, TypeScript, and backend services with NestJS and Socket.IO, also developing tools and contributing to the integration of Tailwind CSS.

Now at Webpooyan, I work on performance and security — optimizing Docker/Nginx setups, building a dual-calendar system (Jalali & Gregorian), and upgrading real-time features using Laravel Reverb.

I'm passionate about clean architecture, problem-solving, and lifelong learning. Always open to new challenges!
